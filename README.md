# MSPBIDEV
## Microsoft Power BI Developer and Architect Professional Certificate

### Professional Certificate - 4 course series

Power BI is one of the most in-demand analytics platforms in the world and organizations urgently need skilled professionals who can design secure, scalable solutions that deliver real business impact. This advanced-level certificate prepares you to specialize as Power BI Developers and Architects, capable of building and governing enterprise-grade analytics systems.Whether you are already working in BI or looking to grow into a more technical and strategic role, this program helps you deepen your expertise in data modeling, DAX, performance optimization, advanced visualizations, deployment, and governance. You’ll learn how to integrate Power BI with Microsoft Fabric and Azure data services, implement Row-Level Security, automate deployments, and build high-performing interactive experiences that drive better decisions.Throughout the program, you’ll complete hands-on projects that mirror real business needs, including building optimized semantic models, creating dynamic dashboards with advanced DAX, and architecting a full enterprise analytics solution.By the end of this certificate, you’ll be able to design, implement, and lead Power BI solutions across an organization, unlocking the next stage in your analytics career.This certificate  may support career growth toward roles including: Power BI Developer, BI Engineer, Data Analyst (Power BI), Power BI Solutions Architect, or Analytics Consultant.
